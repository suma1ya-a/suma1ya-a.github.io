Welcome to A Good Girl's Cluebook. This is inspired by the show 'A Good Girl's Guide to Murder'. This show follows seventeen year old Pip Fitz-Amobi (played by Emma Myers), who has decided to write her EPQ on the Andie Bell Murder. This website aims to document Pip's investigation.

Andie Bell Murder

Five years ago, in the small town of Fairview (where Pip lives), popular student Andie Bell mysteriously disappeared and later found dead in a remote area.

Her boyfriend, Sal Singh (above), was quickly convicted as the murder despite his claims of innocence. Sal was sentenced and the case was closed. The weight of his wrongful conviction leads Sal to take his own life.

With the help of Ravi Singh (Sal's brother) and the support of her best friend Cara Ward, Pip aims to prove Sal's innocence.
